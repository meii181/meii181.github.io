About me

Let me introduce myself, I was born and raised in Romania and been based in Copenhagen, Denmark since the age of 19. I do have background in Multimedia Design and Communication thanks to AP degree I obtained back in summer 2021, where I used to take care of photography/videography, UI/UX Design, and Front-end.

I am a creative, curious and ambitious person who most of the time prefers to work individually than in a group team, eager to experience new challenges and opportunities where not only will gain more experience and skills, but also learn new and interesting things. I am half introvert half extrovert as sometimes I do love hanging out with people, but on the other side I prefer to have a 'me' time.
